# YajiTram Ladang Line (YTL)

The YajiTram Ladang Line (YTL) is a one-way looping local tram line of the
[Transport for Yayajima](/rail-networks/tfy) (TfY). This tram line aims to
connect all of the crop farms to the metro station, to ease the farmers to
harvest the crops.
