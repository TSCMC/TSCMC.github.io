# Lacon Main Line

래이컨 본선/レーコン本線<br>
Lacon Main Line is the oldest Lacon line, connecting Spawn and Floating Moonlight City to Lacon Forestry and Lacon's industrial district at Kurageshima.

## Stations

- Lacon Wye Street (Terminal)
- Floating Moonlight City
- Yayajima South
- Yayajima North
- Lacon Forestry
- Nozomigaoka
- Kurageshima
