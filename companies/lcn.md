# Lacon Inc.

This company is a massive conglomerate that has a subsidiary in almost any industry one could imagine.

# Subsidiaries
## Major Subsidiaries
### Lacon Inc.

Although this name is often used to refer to the conglomerate as a whole, it is officially the
name of the holding company that owns everything.<br>
This company is in charge of *owning stuff*. Not just the trademark and subsidiaries but also some key real estate
(like HQ buildings and employee housing), as well as many of the fleet vehicles used by the employees
of the various subsidiaries.<br>
By tradition this company also owns the high-ranking employees' houses. Once a candidate accepts
an offer to become a high-ranking employee, they are instantly paid a certain amout in the form of the company
"purchasing" their current home. Most of them choose to continue living there as "employee housing" which waives
them of property taxes. Once they resign or are fired, the house is sold back to the employee at 1 United Cities cent.

### [Lacon Railways](/rail-networks/lcn)

This is the root of the company, where the conglomerate got its start.
As such, Lacon Railways is not just a mere subsidiary of Lacon Inc., it is a
conglomerate on its own right and holds many subsidiaries.

### Lacon Bus Lines

A supplementary passenger transportation branch to the railways. Although nowhere near as 
extensive as the railways, they operate a somewhat impressive network of long-distance
buses that connect many cities.

### Lacon Construction

This is the unsung hero of the Lacon family. Lacon Construction is in charge of almost all
designing, engineering, and construction work for Lacon-owned buildings and infrastructure.
They are also a major real estate developer in [FMC](/areas/ucl/fmcity).

### Lacon Real Estate

Unlike what the name implies, this company isn't directly involved in making profits from real estate.
Rather, other subsidiaries of Lacon buy their real estate under the name of Lacon Real Estate as a way
to easily catalogue and manage real estate owned by the conglomerate.<br>
Most of the land owned by Lacon Real Estate is either land for railways and stations for Lacon Railways
or bus terminals that Lacon Bus Lines owns.<br>
Subsidiaries pay "deposlit" and "rent" to Lacon Real Estate, which is what Lacon Real Estate uses to
purchase the lands as requested by the various branches of the company. Once a land has been purchased by
a subsidiary, the profits made from that land is considered to be from that subsidiary even though it
occurs through Lacon Real Estate.<br>
However, it should be noted that a significant portion of Lacon's real estate holdings are owned by other
subsidiaries, most notably the Lacon Tower, the Second Lacon Tower, and Lacon Banho Tower are owned
directly by Lacon Inc., rather than being owned through Lacon Real Estate.
