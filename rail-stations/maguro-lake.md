# Maguro Lake

<sub>クロマグロがとんでくる</sub>マグロ湖<br>
Maguro Lake is a small station on the Dongbu Line,
which serves a small fishing lake of the same name.
The name was inspired by krnb's "Bluefin Tuna Comes Flying" (linked below).
