# New Beijing

新北京<br>
**New Beijing**, abbreviated as **NBJ**, is the most populous and capital city
in the Tianfu Alliance, formerly the Central Union of Maritime States, located
in the Central Sea. It comprises of 5 sectors: Bisexual Reef, Huanglong,
Beijiao, Quyouwei, and Xiaoxing. New Beijing is a centre of finance, commerce,
and technology, and is the headquarters of T-corp.
